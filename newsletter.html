Community · Newsletter

A quarterly update.

The Tesfa Grid newsletter is a quarterly digest of programme updates — new preprints, seminar announcements, responses to open problems, and occasional commentary on developments in number theory relevant to the research.

Subscribe

Four issues per year. No tracking, no sharing with third parties. Unsubscribe with one click.

Your subscription will be confirmed by email. The newsletter operates under a simple one-to-many mailing list — your address is not displayed to other subscribers.

What to expect

Research updates
New preprints, revisions to existing work, and brief summaries of their findings. Volume III will be announced here when complete.
Seminar announcements
Upcoming online and in-person talks, with connection details and topic summaries.
Open problem status
Partial results received from correspondents, proofs of open problems (with full credit to the contributor), and new problems being added to the list.
Commentary
Occasional short essays on methodology, the relationship between the Tesfa Grid and other results in number theory, and the interpretation of the empirical findings.
